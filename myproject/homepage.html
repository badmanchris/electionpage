Student union government is comprised of 10 elected representatives and is chiefly responsible for exercising the legislative powers and duties of shared governance as stipulated by the School Governing Council. Student Council Representatives serve their constituents through student advocacy and by reviewing and recommending changes to campus policy pertaining to student life, services, and interests. Students vote for Student union government representatives based on their college/school status. For example, students in the College of Engineering vote for students within that college.
